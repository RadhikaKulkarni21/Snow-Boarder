# Snow-Boarder
 2D side scroller game with ups and downs, goal is to reach at the end of the stage. 
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bcd40a37-8666-4169-bea4-f27a5051be46)

[Gameplay](https://youtu.be/vAUN166N7GA)

Concepts covered:
1) Cinemachine Virtual camera
2) Sprite Shapes
3) Surface Effectors
4) Particle Effectors
5) Audio Effects

Explanation:
- A 2D side scroller game with ups and downs, goal is to reach at the end of the stage.
- The pre-defined input manager handles the movement with additional movement added in forward and backward so that the player doesn't flip
- Particle system is placed at the end of the snowboard to show friction between the ground and board as well as sound effects.
- As soon as the player flips downwards, after three second delay the level restarts.
- Particle and sound effect also placed as soon as the player hits the flag pole to end the level.
